About Prarabdham

Why This Exists

Prarabdham was built for one reason — to serve this knowledge honestly and completely, without expecting anything in return.

Jyotish is one of the most profound systems of self knowledge ever developed. It is also one of the most misrepresented. Fear, commercial interest, and incomplete understanding have turned what is genuinely a map of the soul's journey into a source of anxiety, superstition, and dependency for millions of people.

This needed to change.

The idea for Prarabdham came from a personal chart reading — a D60 reading that said this: take complex knowledge and make it useful for others. That is what this site is. Nothing more, nothing less.

What This Site Asks of You

This site asks two things.

Patience — with the knowledge, with yourself, and with the process of genuine understanding. Jyotish is not a quick answer system. It is a lifetime companion if approached honestly.

Viveka — discrimination. The capacity to read what is written here and sit with it honestly rather than accepting or rejecting it based on what you hoped to hear. The ground reality of this shastra is available to anyone who approaches it with genuine inquiry rather than expectation.

Our Commitment

This site is free. It will remain free. Every chart, every description, every piece of guidance — available to everyone without cost, without condition, and without agenda.

But free does not mean casual. This knowledge carries real weight and real responsibility. We ask you not to treat it as entertainment or time pass. The soul's transformation is not a side project — it is the central tension of existence itself. The observer in you already knows this. This site simply tries to serve that knowing honestly.

A Direct Word About How to Use This

Do not hold anything here too lightly. In difficult times, what seems small or philosophical often becomes the thing that carries you through. The understanding you skip past today may be exactly what you need later.

And equally — do not hold anything too tightly. Going too deep into any single interpretation, any single fear, any single prediction creates its own obstruction. The chart is a map, not a verdict. Hold it with enough seriousness to learn from it and enough lightness to remain free.

Learn to be present with what is. Conscious engagement with the moment — not anxiety about the future or grief about the past — is where genuine understanding begins. That is also where bliss begins.

Made with purpose, free forever.
